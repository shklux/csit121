MissionFit Website Usability Evaluation

1. What is the URL of the website?

https://missionfit.org/

2. What is the name of the website?

MissionFit

3. Who is the site’s target audience?

The target audience of MissionFit appears to be individuals and communities interested in fitness, health, and well-being, particularly in underserved areas.

4. How is the site organized?

The site is organized in a clear and straightforward manner, with a main navigation bar at the top linking to key sections such as "About," "Programs," "Contact," and "Donate."

5. Which CRAP Design Principle does the site use? Provide at least one example.

The site employs the Proximity principle well. Related information, such as program descriptions, are grouped closely together, which helps users understand the relationships between content.

6. What is the Audit Score according to the Accessibility Checker?

The accessibility audit score is 92/100, with a few minor issues related to contrast and missing alt text on images.

7. What is the site’s effectiveness? Does it support users in completing action accurately?

The site is effective in guiding users to key areas such as learning about programs and making donations. The MissionFit website effectively supports users in completing key actions such as navigating to program information, registering for events, or finding contact details. Links are clear and call-to-actions are prominent.

8. What is the site’s efficiency? Can users can performs tasks quickly?

The MissionFit website is efficient, users can quickly navigate the site and perform tasks like contacting the organization or donating within a few clicks.

9. How is the engagement? Is it pleasant to use and appropriate for its industry/topic?

The site is engaging, with an inspiring mission and clear visuals that resonate with its purpose. However,it is pleasant to use and appropriate for its industry because the site could improve by offering more interactive elements like videos or testimonials.

10. Recommendations for Improvement

To improve the site, I recommend enhancing contrast on certain elements for better readability, and adding alt text to all images to improve accessibility for users with screen readers.
